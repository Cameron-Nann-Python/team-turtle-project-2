## River Ride – Explore the Loxahatchee

## Overview
- Our project is a virtual boat ride through a Florida river environment inspired by the natural waterways and rivers found throughout Florida.
- Players will travel in a boat along the river and observe various wildlife and nature environments.
- While traveling, players will encounter animals such as turtles, birds, manatees, and other animals that will make each ride feel unique.

## Audience
- Florida residents
- Tourists interested in Florida's nature
- Students learning about Florida ecosystems
- People who cannot normally access bodies of water for diversion

## Expected Outcomes
- Provide an immersive and relaxing virtual experience of a Florida inspired river in an interactive and engaging way.
- Increase awareness of Florida's ecosystems such as rivers, springs, and wildlife.
- Encourage conservation of natural environments.
- A higher sense of connectedness and relaxation when viewing future aquatic scenes.
